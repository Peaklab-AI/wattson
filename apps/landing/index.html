Wattson

Tesla Powerwall Status in Mac Menu Bar

Shows your Tesla Powerwall's charge, consumption, and power source in the Mac menu bar.

$ curl -fsSL https://wattson.peaklab.ai/install.sh | bash

Paste into Terminal: installs to /Applications and launches Wattson.

Always visible in your Mac menu bar

Wattson signs you in with Tesla's official OAuth login, then stores your access token in the Mac Keychain, never on our servers.
